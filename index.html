Who am I?

I was born in 1992 in the Philippines. I have been a marketing professional for over 7 years and I have worked mainly in the Telecommunications and Fitness Industries. In 2015, I decided to quit my full-time job to be able to fully pursue my fitness management start-up. While working on this, I decided to acquire an MBA degree, which I received in 2018. As of now, I do consulting jobs with different companies and individuals to help them improve their strategies and executions in marketing, operations, and general management.
